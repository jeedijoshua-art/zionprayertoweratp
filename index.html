About Us

Our Story  ·  What We Believe

Our Story

Rooted in the True Vine

🍇

Zion Prayer Tower ATP is a Spirit-filled, community-rooted church in Avinashi, Tamil Nadu. We are a people grafted into the True Vine — Jesus Christ — drawing life, strength, and purpose from Him alone.

Just as the Gardener tends the vineyard with care, pruning what is unfruitful and binding the broken branches, God has been faithfully at work in this congregation — restoring, renewing, and reviving hearts.

Every person who walks through our doors is welcomed — the weary, the broken, the seeker, and the faithful. You have a home here.

"Every branch in me that does not bear fruit He takes away, and every branch that does bear fruit He prunes, that it may bear more fruit."
— John 15:2 (ESV)

What We Believe

The Branches We Stand On

✝

The Word

We stand on the Holy Bible as the inspired, inerrant Word of God — the foundation of all we believe.

Prayer

Prayer is the lifeblood of this church. We are a house of prayer — interceding for our city and the nations.

The Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day work of the Holy Spirit — His gifts, power, and transforming presence.

Community

We are a family. We bear one another's burdens and grow together as one body in Christ.
